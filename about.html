About J. Max Hamidi

Thirty years of judgment, not just transactions.

J. Max Hamidi, CCIM, is a commercial real estate broker and advisor based in Germantown, Tennessee. With over thirty years in the business, Max has built his practice on a simple principle: know the deal behind the property.

His path through real estate began in retail: gas stations, convenience stores, and strip centers. That hands-on experience with operator-led properties gave him an understanding of commercial assets that goes beyond spreadsheet math.

Earlier in his career, Max identified and acquired Circle K sites across Tennessee, Mississippi, and Alabama, helped expand Perkins Family Restaurants with 13 new Perkins locations in the Northeast, sharpened his brokerage craft at Marcus & Millichap in Memphis, and later served through Sperry Commercial Global Affiliates before eXp Commercial.

Over time, Max expanded into loan packaging: organizing borrower and property facts into bank-ready presentations that give lenders confidence. He does not originate loans; he prepares the conversation so it leads somewhere useful.

Today, Max manages properties for a select group of investors, handles leasing and rent collection, and continues to advise on acquisitions and dispositions. He works with a small team of mentees at eXp Commercial, passing along the judgment that only comes from decades of real transactions.

"I enjoy what I do. People ask when I'll retire — to do what? This is what I love."

Philosophy

Personal. Selective. Experienced.

Max does not take every deal that comes through the door. He is selective about the opportunities he pursues because his clients deserve focused attention, not divided loyalty. His work is guided by Vision, Fun, and thoughtful, cooperative, and ethical practices. When Max takes on a project, he brings the full weight of his experience to the table.
