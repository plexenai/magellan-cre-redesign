About Max

J. Max Hamidi, CCIM brings operator judgment to commercial real estate.

Max's advisory style is personal and direct. Clients and referral partners get memorable guidance, a practical read on the opportunity, and a clear sense of whether the next step is worth taking.

The Path

Retail, restaurant, brokerage, and owner-side experience all show up in the details.

Retail Roots

Circle K location acquisition

Max began in commercial real estate by identifying and acquiring locations for the Circle K brand across Tennessee, Mississippi, and Alabama, with an emphasis on larger convenience and fueling stations.

Restaurant Growth

Perkins Family Restaurants

As a franchise regional manager for Perkins Family Restaurants, Max specialized in site selection, operations, and market growth — helping add 13 new Perkins locations across the Northeast.

Brokerage Foundation

Marcus & Millichap and beyond

Max joined Marcus & Millichap in Memphis in 2017, then opened his own firm and built a referral-driven commercial real estate practice where decades-old relationships still send him new clients.

Today

The Magellan Group

As CEO and managing broker — with experience leading Magellan Group under Sperry Commercial Global Affiliates and now affiliated with eXp Commercial — Max helps clients acquire retail properties, develop strip centers, lease and manage assets, and evaluate commercial opportunities.
