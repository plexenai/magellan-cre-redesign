About Max

J. Max Hamidi, CCIM brings operator judgment to commercial real estate.

Max's advisory style is personal and direct. He wants clients and referral partners to get memorable guidance, a practical read on the opportunity, and a clear sense of whether the next step is worth taking.

Max's Background

Retail, restaurant, brokerage, and owner-side experience all show up in the details.

Retail roots

Circle K location acquisition

Max began in commercial real estate by identifying and acquiring locations for the Circle K brand across Tennessee, Mississippi, and Alabama, with an emphasis on larger convenience and fueling stations.

Restaurant growth

Perkins Family Restaurants

Before commercial brokerage became his main lane, Max worked as a franchise regional manager for Perkins Family Restaurants, specializing in site selection, operations, and market growth. He helped add 13 new Perkins locations in the Northeast.

Brokerage foundation

Marcus & Millichap and beyond

Max joined Marcus & Millichap in Memphis in 2017, then opened his own firm and continued building a referral-driven commercial real estate practice.

Current role

Sperry Commercial Global Affiliates

Max serves as CEO and managing broker with Sperry Commercial Global Affiliates, Magellan Group, helping clients acquire retail properties, develop strip centers, lease and manage assets, and evaluate commercial opportunities.
