Deal Matrix

Is this deal a good fit?

Before you send a deal Max's way, use this matrix to check if it aligns with his expertise and criteria. Better referrals lead to better outcomes for everyone.

Good Fit

These are the types of deals and clients Max works best with:

  • Commercial properties near seven figures or above
  • Retail assets (gas stations, convenience stores, strip centers)
  • Investors seeking property management
  • Borrowers needing loan packaging for commercial deals
  • Sellers of commercial property in the Memphis region
  • Referral partners with qualified commercial opportunities
  • Portfolio growth for existing investors
  • Landlord and tenant representation (commercial)

Not a Fit

Max is selective about the deals he takes on. These typically do not align:

  • Residential properties under $500K
  • International transactions
  • Raw land without commercial intent
  • Deals requiring immediate closing (under 30 days)
  • Properties outside the Memphis/Mid-South region
  • Speculative development without financing

Have a deal that fits?

If your opportunity matches the criteria above, Max would like to hear about it. Start with the basics and he will let you know if it deserves the next conversation.
