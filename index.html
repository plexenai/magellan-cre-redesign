The Deal Matrix

A simple filter for the right conversation.

Max is selective because the work is advisory. The best conversations start with enough substance for him to add real value.

Strong fit

  • Retail properties — gas stations, convenience stores, strip centers
  • Investor-owned assets that need leasing or management
  • Loan packages headed for bank conversations
  • Transactions near seven figures and above

Bring the details

  • Property address, asset type, and current use
  • Your role — owner, borrower, investor, tenant, referral
  • Estimated value, lease status, and timing

Not the first priority

  • Small residential searches
  • Leads without ownership or borrower context
  • Deals where Max can't help at the level the client deserves
