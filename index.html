Deal Fit

A useful filter before the first call.

The best conversations start with clear intent. This preview gives future visitors a simple way to understand whether Magellan is the right advisory fit.

Good fit

  • Commercial retail assets and investor-owned properties
  • Acquisitions, sales, leasing, and management needs
  • Serious financing conversations for commercial projects
  • Referral partners with clear property or borrower context

Probably not a fit

  • Small residential house searches
  • Speculative requests without requirements or direction
  • Very small transactions where commercial advisory effort does not make sense
  • Inquiries without a clear owner, investor, borrower, or property objective
