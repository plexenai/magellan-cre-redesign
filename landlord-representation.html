Services · Landlord Representation

Leasing strategy for owners who care about the tenant mix and the long-term asset.

Landlord representation is about more than filling space. It means understanding the property, the market, the ownership goals, and the kind of tenant that supports value.

01

Space & Market Readiness

Review available space, condition, rents, tenant mix, signage, visibility, access, operating costs, and the story a tenant will need to believe.

02

Tenant Targeting

Focus outreach on tenants who fit the property's use, economics, neighborhood, and owner goals rather than chasing every possible inquiry.

03

Lease Conversations

Support lease terms, renewal questions, tenant improvements, timing, and negotiations so the owner can make a grounded decision.
