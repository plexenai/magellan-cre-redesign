# Magellan CRE Redesign Brief

## Approved Direction

Founder-Led Advisor, made even more personal.

The redesigned site should lead with J. Max Hamidi, CCIM as the trusted commercial real estate advisor behind The Magellan Group. The site should feel seasoned, relationship-driven, practical, warm, selective, and highly experienced.

## Main Problem To Solve

The current site has useful raw information, but it feels too generic and template-like. Max wants the site cleaned up into memorable, focused points that feel like him, not like broad brokerage copy.

## Audience

Serious commercial real estate investors, owners, borrowers, business owners, and referral partners in Germantown, Memphis, and nearby markets.

Priority niches:

- Retail real estate
- Gas stations
- Convenience stores
- Strip centers
- Commercial acquisitions and sales
- Leasing
- Property management
- Bank-ready financing conversations

## Positioning

Max is not simply listing properties. He helps people understand whether a commercial real estate deal makes sense, organizes the details behind the deal, and helps serious owners, investors, and borrowers move toward the right next step.

He also packages commercial loans/deals for bank conversations, manages investor properties, leases space, collects rent, markets vacancies, and helps investors add stores or strip centers to their portfolios.

## Design Direction

Editorial Advisory / Personal Commercial Real Estate.

Use a warm, established, human design language. Avoid generic SaaS, generic brokerage templates, stock skyline imagery, oversized empty hero cards, and long SEO filler.

Palette:

- Deep charcoal/navy: `#17202A`
- Warm parchment: `#F6F1E8`
- Brass/gold: `#B08A3C`
- Muted sage: `#6F8178`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Text gray: `#4B535B`

Typography:

- Editorial serif or display face for major headlines
- Crisp sans-serif for body copy
- Highly readable, established, not trendy

Signature elements:

- Personal note or quote block from Max
- Deal-fit checklist cards
- Field-note-style service lanes
- Subtle map/site-plan line motifs
- Relational advisor cards
