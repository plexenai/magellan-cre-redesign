Services · Seller Representation

A disciplined sales process for owners who need more than a listing.

Seller representation means helping an owner understand value, prepare the property, shape the story, qualify buyers, and negotiate without losing sight of the owner's real objective.

01

Readiness Before Market

Clarify ownership goals, operating facts, documents, lease details, current condition, timing, and pricing expectations before buyer conversations start.

02

Positioning & Outreach

Frame the property around the buyer it should attract, not a generic brochure. The goal is serious attention from qualified commercial prospects.

03

Negotiation & Closing Coordination

Keep the process organized through offers, diligence, lender questions, contract terms, and closing steps.
